About the Project

The Smart Shift Swap System is designed to eliminate the inefficiencies and conflicts involved in manual shift swapping. Instead of random requests, our system uses predefined criteria to determine the most suitable replacement. By analyzing availability, past swap frequency, overtime history, and current workload, Shift-UP recommends coworkers who are not only available but also fair choices, preventing overworking or underutilizing employees.
